28th Dec 1612

Galileo & Neptune

There is evidence that Neptune was seen and recorded by Galileo Galilei in 1613, where his drawings show that he observed Neptune on December 28, 1612, Galileo which he mistook for a fixed star when it appeared very close to Jupiter in the night sky.

28th Dec 1832

John C. Calhoun resigns

On December 28th 1832, with only a few months remaining in his second term, John C. Calhoun resigned as vice president and entered the Senate. He was the first ever vice president to resign from office.
